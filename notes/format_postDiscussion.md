## Limitations in Measurements

You might ask yourself, why can't we just measure the mass of black holes and see this gap.
First issue, previous limitation in how large a black hole we can measure
    - Door opened by gravitational wave measurements
Second issue, contamination by second generation blackholes
    - For example, 1G + 1G merged blackholes will exhibit a total mass inside the bband gap
Third issue, measurement errors prevelant with such indiscrete techniques
    - Toby's points
